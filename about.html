About Harvest Area

Our story and what makes us special

Our Story

Harvest Area is a farm visitor attraction located between Edinburgh and Carlisle. We offer a fun day out for families with our petting zoo, play park, cafe, shop, and walking trails.

What We Offer

  • Farm shop with local produce and plants
  • Petting zoo with friendly animals
  • Cafe serving homemade food
  • Adventure playground
  • Walking trails

Quick Facts

  • Established: 1952
  • Visitor attraction since: 2018
  • Size: 150 acres
