Welcome to Harvest Area

A farm visitor attraction between Edinburgh and Carlisle

Your Countryside Escape

Harvest Area is the perfect destination for families, garden enthusiasts, and travelers looking for a memorable day out in the beautiful scottish borders.

What We Offer

  • Petting Zoo - Meet and interact with our farm animals
  • Farm Shop - Fresh local produce and nice plants
  • Cafe - Homemade food prepared with local ingredients
  • Play Park - Adventure playground for children
  • Walking Trails - Explore our nice view paths
